Our Story

Founded on trust, built for the long term

Dimora — Italian for "dwelling" — began with a simple frustration: buying property in our markets was too often opaque, stressful and uncertain. We set out to change that.

Today we connect discerning buyers and investors with exceptional homes and high-yield assets across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Dubai, London and Accra. Every listing is vetted, every title verified, every projection grounded in real data.

Our Pedal program takes it further — fully managing short-let properties so owners earn dependable monthly income without lifting a finger.
